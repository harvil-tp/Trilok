FT Flume

FT FLUME

MAJI INFRA Precast FT Flume is a large U Shape Drain designed as water way product. FT Flume is alongitudinal drain and its waffle walls are designed JIS T-25 i.e. 10 KN/m2 live load.

FT Flume is available in the following sizes.

Width: 900 ~ 2000 mm

Height: 900 ~ 1800 mm

Pun on Lid of FT Flume is designed for JIS (T-4) i.e. for pedestrian load and it is available inStandard Lid & No Hole option.

Maji Infra Precast Drop Lid U Shape is designed as per JIS (T-25). FT Flume U Shape can also be used as Utility Duct / Cable Duct.
